Music Box Theatre Logo

Project Details

  • Student Designer
  • 4121 Identity Systems
  • Length: 5 weeks

Objectives

The goal of this project was to research an organization, and use that research as a basis for designing a new logo for it. This was a group project, and I researched Music Box Theatre alongside 3 other designers. However, we all developed our logos separately, and in the end, mine was chosen to be the final logo. While working on this smaller logo, we were simultaneously researching our chosen organization for our main Identity System project, and you can view mine here.
