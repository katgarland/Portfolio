Composting Poster and Booklets

Project Details

  • Student Designer
  • 4011 Advanced Typography
  • Length: 5 weeks

Objectives

The goal of this project was to produce an educational poster that promotes composting, using nothing but type and up to three colors. Our main focus was to convey information in a way that is easy for readers to digest, with clear hierarchies and reading orders.

Katherine Garland
