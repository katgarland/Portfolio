MoMA Poster

Project Details

  • Student Designer
  • 3011 Intermediate Type
  • Length: 4 weeks

Objectives

The goal of this poster was to present a series of photography exhibitions at the Museum of Modern Art in New York. I chose to base my composition off of a camera lens, with additional information pertaining to each exhibition located near the bottom of the poster.

Katherine Garland
