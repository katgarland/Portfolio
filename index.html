Hello! I'm Katherine.

I'm a visual designer based in Chicago, IL. My belief is that by carefully controlling and applying aesthetics, we can curate the world into an overall better place. I am interested in all aspects of design, and my recent focus has been on UI/UX.

I believe the best way to create a connection with my audience is through strong storytelling, and my design work strives to create distinct, compelling experiences that clearly communicate brand information in a way that is memorable and impactful. After all, the best medium is not print or pixels–it is the viewer’s mind.

I am currently planning to complete my BFA with concentration in Visual Communications in the spring of 2025, and I am looking for work opportunities! Please reach out to me if my work interests you.
